**Session 7 Log**

---

**Opening Vignette**

Minka Brinberg and two mercenaries suit up in the hub, relaxed and unhurried. They cycle through the airlock and stroll toward the eastern residential cluster, Minka's cabin visible ahead. She is thinking about Sandra's message and what she is going to say to her.

---

**Beat 1 — First Blood**

The circle holds position at the lean-to as the escort arrives. One mercenary follows Minka inside; the other takes up a loose position outside the airlock, back partially to the lean-to. Gerald signals the circle and thumbs the jammer. Three weapons open up on the outside merc. He goes down without a sound. Someone noticed the comms going down though, and the airlock starts to cycle again. Someone is coming out. *(Enter the Fray +shadow, banked add: weak hit. In control.)*

---

**Beat 2 — Repositioning**

Gerald signals Sandra to the eastern airlock with a hand gesture and rotates with Davel to either side of the main door. The circle repositions cleanly. The airlock indicator shifts — the cycle is complete. *(Gain Ground +wits: strong hit. Progress 3/10. +1 add banked.)*

---

**Beat 3 — The Second Merc**

The outer door unseals. Gerald is already moving — he forces through the airlock threshold before the merc can raise his weapon, Davel a few steps behind. The merc goes down in the threshold. Inside, Minka has gone completely still. *(Strike +iron, banked add: strong hit. Progress 9/10.)*

---

**Beat 4 — Contained**

Gerald shoves the body inside and advances. Sandra comes through the eastern airlock a moment later. Minka backs against the far wall, terrified. Gerald identifies himself through his suit speakers — master at arms, Steady Return, here to get her out. Outside, Davel is visible through the viewport dragging the first body toward the lean-to. *(Take Decisive Action: strong hit. Objective complete.)*

---

**Beat 5 — Minka**

Minka attempts to call for help — the jammer swallows it. Gerald's rifle comes up. Sandra steps forward and shoots the comms pickup off Minka's helmet, then asks her "why, Minka?" in a tortured voice.

Minka talks. She speaks about what is buried under the moon, about legacy and discovery, building the case she has been constructing in her head for days. Sandra listens without expression. When Minka finishes the cabin is quiet. Sandra shoots her. *(Endure Stress +heart: miss with match. Sandra makes the wrong decision, and it's worse than that — she is inconsolable, unable to return to the settlement.)*

---

**Beat 6 — Talking Sandra Down**

Gerald curses. Sandra turns the sidearm on him, barely present. *"What the fuck did you make me do?"* Gerald advances on her, hands visible, talking her down until the barrel drops and she lets him take the weapon. It costs him. Davel appears in the main airlock and takes in the room without a word. *(Compel +heart: weak hit. Endure Stress -1. Spirit 4 → 3.)*

---

**Beat 7 — Dispersal**

Gerald tells Davel to take Sandra to the hidden compartment — she cannot go back into the settlement in this state. Davel puts a hand on her arm and gets her moving through the eastern airlock. Gerald pulls the jammer from under the porch. Comms snap back on. He tells Carl to get inside, normal pace, normal face. Carl acknowledges and asks no questions.

Gerald makes the crossing back to the shuttle, programs it for an autonomous return flight to the Steady Return, and steps back outside to watch it lift. It clears the ridge and is gone. An outside agent came, did something, and left. The settlement had nothing to do with it.

Gerald stands alone on the surface with a rifle, a sidearm, and a jammer in his pocket. He sets off on foot toward the mines.

---

**Beat 8 — The Walk Begins**

Gerald moves through the terrain steadily, using the ridgelines, keeping the settlement behind him. At some point he becomes aware that no one is coming. The settlement lights drop below the horizon. He keeps walking. *(Undertake an Expedition +edge: miss, momentum burned 10 → 2 for strong hit. The Walk to the Mines 2/10.)*

---

**End State**

Gerald is on foot moving toward the mining infrastructure. Sandra is hidden in Davel's compartment, non-functional. Carl is back inside the settlement. Hamzi's status is unknown. Two raiders and Minka Brinberg are dead. The raiders know something happened at the cabin. They do not yet know whether it came from inside or outside the settlement.

**Mechanical updates:** Momentum 2. Spirit 3. Health 5. Supply 4. Vow progress 2/10 unchanged. Davel connection progress 6/10. Sandra connection progress 3/10. The Walk to the Mines expedition 2/10.
