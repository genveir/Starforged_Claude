**SESSION 10**

**Opening Vignette**

The Steady Return receives a hail — voice only, blank channel ID. A demand to cease commando operations or face decisive action. Graff's eyebrows lift slightly. The raiders have just confirmed Gerald is still loose and that they can't do anything about it. He closes the channel without responding.

---

**Beat 1 — The Strategy Call**

The circle debates Maria's proposal to blow the cable. Gerald is not opposed — the miners have weapons and will fight if pressed — and Davel agrees. Carl pushes back: the raiders have a ship, and nothing stops them shifting orbit a short distance to support their people on the ground. The cable plan stalls on that question. Then Maria proposes using the ore launcher to shoot the raider out of the sky. Silence.

---

**Beat 2 — Comms Close**

Gerald shuts down the ore launcher idea immediately — dumb munitions against a maneuvering ship. Carl is right. The cable can still go, but only coordinated with Graff, with the shuttle moving first to sell the commando fiction. That means comms. Gerald asks Hamzi if he has any ideas. "Leave it to me." Comms close. A few minutes later Maria appears at the rest area door. She's found him a place to bunk.

---

**Beat 3 — Hamzi**

Hamzi settles into a maintenance corridor with a service terminal and runs a diagnostic, bypassing the raiders' auditing suite from below. He finds the tap — not a block but a redirect. Everything Gerald tried to send to Graff was copied and forwarded to the raider's ship. They've known someone on the surface was trying to make contact since the first attempt.

---

**Beat 4 — Hamzi**

Hamzi relocates to an empty equipment locker and spends the better part of an hour writing a software redirect below the layer the raiders can see, routing Gerald's outbound traffic to the backup dish at the complex. He finds one inelegant line and rewrites it for no practical reason. He composes a short message with deployment instructions and sits in the dark smiling at nothing in particular.

---

**Beat 5 — Bunking Down**

The rest area is small, four bunks, Maria already claimed one. Gerald sits on the edge of his bunk and finds himself not reaching for anything. He thinks about the people. Carl and Hamzi, reliable men he trusts more now than before. Maria, a kid from Spinfeld grown up and a long way from home, who dropped her guard the moment she placed him. Davel, who came out of nowhere and had his back at every step without ever making it about himself. Sandra, who he still carries guilt over, but whose way of handling it — not angry, just accepting — he recognises and respects deeply. Good people. Better than this moon deserves. He lies back and lets sleep take him. *(Deepen the Connection — Davel: troublesome → dangerous, progress 9/10 → 6/10. Deepen the Connection — Sandra: troublesome → dangerous, progress 6/10 → 4/10.)*

---

**Beat 6 — Morning**

Gerald wakes to his handset buzzing against his chest — he'd fallen asleep with it in his hand. Hamzi's message is smug, thorough, and entirely earned: the tap has been bypassed in software, traffic rerouted to the backup dish at the complex, deployment instructions attached. Maria is already lacing her boots. She got a message too and knows exactly where the dish is. Getting it outside without anyone asking questions, she notes, is going to be interesting.

---

**Beat 7 — The Raider**

They round a corner and find one of the assigned raiders directly in their path. Before Gerald can react Maria grabs him by the collar and kisses him. This is the most awkward moment in Gerald's life to date. He stares at a fixed point past her ear with the focused expression of a man defusing an explosive while the raider backs off embarrassed and moves on. Maria wipes her mouth and resumes walking. Gerald stays against the wall for a moment. He will be having words with her later. *(Undertake an Expedition +shadow: miss. Compel +heart: strong hit. +1 momentum.)*

---

**Beat 8 — The Equipment Lockup**

The rest of the route is completely uneventful. No raiders, no supervisors, nobody to dodge. Gerald spends most of it quietly relitigating the kiss. Maria asks if he had a better plan. He did not. The dish is exactly where Hamzi said it would be, racked neatly in the equipment lockup. Maria pats it like an old friend. Gerald decides some arguments are not worth having on a moon with a ton of armed mercenaries on it. *(Undertake an Expedition +shadow: strong hit with a match. Mark 4 progress — expedition at 4/10. Momentum burned 8 → 2, resets to 2.)*

---

**Beat 9 — Loading the Buggy**

Loading the dish goes smoothly until it doesn't. Low gravity makes the antenna array swing wide and Gerald's boot catches the running board at the wrong moment. The dish comes down on the outside edge of his foot. He claims to be fine. Walking with intention, not hurt. Nobody in the equipment yard stops them. *(Undertake an Expedition +heart: weak hit. Mark 3 progress — expedition at 7/10. Health 4 → 3. Momentum 2 → 1.)*

---

**Beat 10 — Out the Gate and Up the Wall**

Maria drives past the raider at the complex gate without slowing down. Nobody looks twice at a buggy on a maintenance run. The service track switchbacks up the crater wall, the complex shrinking below. Gerald rides with his foot propped against the dash, watching it go — peaceful from up here, same as the first time, same lie. The relay tower appears on the rim above them. Maria kills the drive at its base and looks at him. "Your boss better pick up." *(Undertake an Expedition +heart: strong hit. Mark 3 progress — expedition at 10/10. Momentum 1 → 3.)*

---

**Beat 11 — Hey Boss**

Maria mounts the dish on the relay tower's secondary bracket while Gerald runs the hardline connection from the rock shelf, bad foot stretched out, following Hamzi's instructions. When the status light goes green he opens a channel. Static, then live static. Then Graff's voice — steady as always, but warmer than usual. Gerald closes his eye for a moment. *"Hey boss. Sorry I've been out of touch."* A pause. *"Gerald. Good to hear your voice. Talk to me."* *(Finish the Expedition: strong hit. Mark 1 tick on discoveries legacy track.)*

---

**End State**

Gerald is at the crater rim relay tower, on a live channel with Graff for the first time since landing. The missile plan is no longer blocked. Maria is with him. The complex garrison does not know anything happened.
