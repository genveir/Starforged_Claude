**SESSION 12**

**Opening Vignette**

The bridge of the raider ship, dim and quiet. The officer in the captain's chair, brooding. His lieutenant reports a shuttle launch from the Steady Return, inbound on the surface, untraceable in the sensor interference. The officer orders Aran to prepare for a commando strike on the food storage or the jail, has Tom check the tap, and intensifies patrols — pairs only, no singles. The fiction is holding perfectly. *(Begin a Session: vignette spotlights a danger. +1 momentum, 8 → 9.)*

---

**Beat 1 — Watching the Magazine**

Gerald and Maria move through the complex toward the explosives magazine, playing it as a routine stroll. They reach an observation point behind an equipment stack and find the magazine occupied — a full work crew of eight or ten miners restocking the shaft heads for the next rotation, a job Maria estimates will run another hour. Not a hostile situation, but a critical timing problem: Paul Ortoff is already descending into the interference layer, and the cable needs to blow before the fiction burns out. *(Face Danger +heart, Maria bonus applied: miss.)*

---

**Beat 2 — Reading the Room**

Gerald asks Maria about the crew. She knows them — angry quietly, the ration crackdown having reached the complex — but the job is being run by Ferris, one of the cooperating shift supervisors. A man who made his calculation early and has been keeping his people's heads down ever since. Useful intelligence, but a real complication standing in the middle of the people Gerald needs. *(Gather Information +wits: weak hit. +1 momentum, 9 → 10.)*

---

**Beat 3 — Gerald Steps Out**

Gerald walks straight up to the group and introduces himself to Ferris as master of arms of the Steady Return. Ferris is stunned. Gerald lays out the situation plainly: the supply ship turned away, the blockade, the ration crackdown, the settlers in jail, three weeks of food left on the moon. Ferris goes pale. The younger miners turn angry. *(Compel +heart, Maria bonus applied: strong hit. Momentum burned 10 → 3.)*

---

**Beat 4 — The Ask**

Gerald tells them the passive option is gone — the timeline has closed it off. He tells them he was a lawman on Sonnet for twenty years, that he knows how to deal with a gang, and that he needs a posse for when the raiders push back. He tells them he knows about the weapons cache. He asks if they can give him explosives and stand ready when called.

---

**Beat 5 — Ferris**

One of the younger miners rounds on Ferris immediately. Ferris absorbs it without flinching and tells Gerald they were wrong, that he'll talk to Bran, and that he'll be there when called. Gerald tells them to hold and keep their heads down — the ship is still the threat that matters. He shakes hands with Ferris and every miner in the group. They load exactly what he needs into the buggy without being asked twice. Maria watches from the edge of it.
