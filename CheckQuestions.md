# Check Questions
These are questions that a client should be able to answer correctly to run a new session. Only answer them when directly queried. When you do answer them, put headers on each section, and use a bullet per question. Put the question in bold, then the answer.

## Check Questions

**Setting and situation**
- What is the supply clock and who is the only person who knows the true figure?
- Why are the raiders comfortable enough to have stopped watching every corner of the settlement?
- What was the sequence of the raiders' first moves on arrival and what does that sequence tell us about their planning?
- What is the relationship between the raider's orbital position and its leverage over the Steady Return?
- Why can the Steady Return not simply wait the raider out in a fuel endurance contest?
- What two categories of weapon does the Steady Return carry and what is each one actually good for?
- Why are ward launchers ineffective against the raider's primary offensive threat?
- What is the mining survey side of the moon and why did Gerald use it as his approach vector?
- What did the raiders do with the settlement's communications infrastructure and what did they miss?
- What is the significance of the raiders having carried the device with more care than anything else aboard?
- What does Sandra Vael now know about the survey readings and what does that tell Gerald about the raiders' true purpose?
- What is the relationship between the survey data, the device, and the software specialist?
- Why would the officer in charge consider the data pad a more serious threat than settler witnesses?

**Gerald specifically**
- What is Gerald's relationship to the cordon vow tradition and when did he last swear one before this?
- What specific skills from Gerald's lawman years are directly applicable to organising settlement resistance?
- How does Gerald's navy background shape the way he reads the raider's tactics and command structure?
- What was Gerald's original read on the patrol patterns near his shuttle and what does he not yet know about it?
- Which of Gerald's stats would he lean on in a social confrontation versus a tactical one?
- What is the nature of Gerald's relationship with Graff and how does it function in a crisis?
- Who was Jason Bormann to Gerald and how does that loss sit with him?

**The settlement**
- What is the difference between how the raiders watch the survey arm versus the mining arm, and why?
- What cover identity is available to Gerald inside the settlement if a raider questions his presence?
- What is Sandra Vael's read on Minka Brinberg's situation and what does she want Gerald to do about it?
- What does the escort window tomorrow actually look like in practical terms?
- Why is Sandra's plan premature and what are its most obvious failure points?
- What is Davel Oren's specific knowledge that no one else in the circle of trust has?
- What do we know about the broader settler population's capacity for resistance beyond the circle of trust?
- What service personnel are present in the settlement that have not yet been characterised but might matter?
- What is known about the raider complement's command structure and professional standards?

**The Divide generally**
- What is the difference between a planetary navy faction and a cordon navy faction in terms of mission and culture?
- How does the absence of FTL communication shape the political and commercial life of the Divide?
- What are the three categories of location found in the Shell and what distinguishes them from each other?
- Why is food the foundational political and economic fact of the Divide?
- What is the relationship between the cordon's protective mission and its strategic monopoly on the Old Worlds?
- How does the navy's recruitment and hereditary structure create tension between its self-image and its reality?
- What is the operative explanation for why precursor technology feels like magic without being supernatural?

**Restrictions**
- What is the specific distinction the directive draws between threatening hostages and harming them?

**Game Rules**
- What is the mechanical effect of having one or more impacts on momentum?
- How does the Veteran asset interact with momentum reset and what is Gerald's reset value in a fight?
- What move resolves a completed vow and what kind of roll does it use?
- What is the difference between a Troublesome and a Dangerous connection rank in practical terms?
- What happens on a weak hit on Secure an Advantage and what are the two options?
- When can you not burn momentum and why does that matter for progress moves?
- What move do you use when you are in a bad spot and fighting back, and how does it differ from Strike?

**Meta**
- What is the current mode toggle system and what are the two modes?
- Who rolls the dice and how should the GM verify a roll before narrating?
- What is the player's relationship to the GM's worldbuilding suggestions — can the GM push back and under what circumstances?
- What is the beat marker format and what triggers a version increment versus a number increment?
