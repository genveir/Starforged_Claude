These are questions that a client should be able to answer correctly to run a new session. Only answer them when directly queried. When you do answer them, use bold section headers and a bullet per question with the question in plain text and the answer following it.

**Setting and situation**
- What is the current supply clock and how many days remain at current consumption rates?
- What time of day does session 6 open and where is Gerald physically located?
- What is the escort window for Minka Brinberg's cabin visit and what does the timing allow the circle to do?
- What did the raiders do to settlement comms on arrival and what did they miss?
- Who is root on the settlement comm system and why do the raiders not know this?
- What is the one operational constraint Hamzi placed on using the secure comm channel?
- What cover identity does Gerald currently have in the settlement's admin system and what needs to be done about it?
- Who could register Gerald as a late arrival and why would it be plausible?
- What is the device the raiders brought, what do the settlers know about it, and what does the narrator know?
- What did Sandra Vael read in the survey data and what does it tell Gerald about the raiders' true purpose?
- Why are the raiders keeping Minka Brinberg alive rather than killing her?
- What is Aran Chrion's rank and what distinguishes him from the other mercenaries?
- What is the relationship between the raider's orbital position and its leverage over the Steady Return?
- Why can the Steady Return not safely approach the settlement?

**Gerald specifically**
- Where did Gerald sleep last night and why did he make that choice?
- What happened on Gerald's walk back to the shuttle and what did it cost him mechanically?
- What are Gerald's current condition meters — health, spirit, supply, and momentum?
- What commitment did Gerald make to Sandra Vael to bring her into the circle and why is it premature?
- What is Gerald's momentum reset value in a fight and what asset produces it?
- What is the current progress on Gerald's formidable vow and what would constitute the next milestone?
- Which of Gerald's stats applies to stealth situations and which to social bluffing and why are they different?

**The settlement**
- Who are the five members of the circle of trust and what does each one contribute?
- What is Davel Oren's specific knowledge that no one else in the circle has?
- What is Hamzi's freedom of movement within the settlement and why do the raiders not supervise him closely?
- What do we know about the broader settler population's capacity for resistance beyond the circle of five?
- What service personnel are present in the settlement that have not yet been characterised but might matter?
- What is known about the raider complement's command structure and professional standards?

**The Divide generally**
- What is the operative explanation for why precursor technology feels like magic without being supernatural?
- How does the absence of FTL communication shape commercial life in the Divide and why does it make courier captains valuable?
- What is the difference between a planetary navy faction and a cordon navy faction in terms of mission?
- What are the three categories of location found in the Shell and what distinguishes them?

**Restrictions**
- What is the specific distinction the directive draws between threatening hostages and harming them?

**Game rules**
- What move resolves a completed vow and what kind of roll does it use?
- What is the mechanical effect of one or more impacts on momentum reset and maximum momentum?
- What happens on a weak hit on Face Danger and who chooses the suffer move?
- What is the difference between Forge a Bond and Develop Your Relationship in mechanical terms?
- What is Hamzi's current connection rank and progress and what triggered the progress mark?
- When can you not burn momentum and why does that matter for progress moves?

**Meta**
- What is the mode toggle system and what are the two modes?
- Who rolls the dice and how should the GM verify a roll before narrating?
- What is the beat marker format and what triggers a version increment versus a number increment?
