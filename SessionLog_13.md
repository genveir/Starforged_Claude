**SESSION 13**

**Opening Vignette**

The cable junction sits on its concrete pad in the grey expanse, unremarkable, the data line running away from it in both directions. A buggy appears in the distance, two mercs on patrol. One complains about the ration crackdown applying to them as well. The other tells him to shut up and stay sharp — the strike team is inbound and he doesn't want to be caught unawares. The buggy rolls on. *(Begin a Session: vignette spotlights a danger. +1 momentum, 4 → 5.)*

---

**Beat 1 — Getting There**

Gerald retrieved his kit from the relay tower on the way out, handing Maria one of the sidearms with a brief practical lesson. On the service track they spotted a dust cloud and pulled over fast. The patrol was maybe four hundred metres out and closing. *(Face Danger +edge: weak hit. Sacrifice Resources -1, supply 3 → 2.)*

**Beat 2 — Setting Up**

Gerald stayed on the boulder rather than coming down — elevation, stable rest, and the mercs below him on flat ground with nowhere to go. Maria took cover at the buggy. Gerald sighted the driver and waited. *(Enter the Fray +shadow: weak hit. In control.)*

**Beat 3 — Waiting**

The hard part was holding still while every instinct said shoot. Gerald talked himself down, let the range close, and was rewarded when the patrol stopped and began to dismount fifty metres out — one still half in the vehicle. *(Secure an Advantage +heart: strong hit with a match. +2 on next Strike.)*

**Beat 4 — First Shot**

Gerald fired and dropped the passenger cleanly. The driver was already moving before the body hit the ground — around the far side of the buggy, into cover, returning controlled fire that sparked close enough off the boulder to send Gerald flat. *(Strike +edge: weak hit. Progress 4/10. In a bad spot.)*

**Beat 5 — Maria**

Gerald called Maria on comms and talked her into flanking the buggy and firing two shots over the hood to draw the driver's attention. She cursed, told him she'd never fired a gun, and moved anyway. It worked. Gerald felt the familiar weight of putting an untrained person into a firefight and made peace with the fact that he couldn't do it alone. *(React Under Fire +heart: weak hit. In control. Endure Stress +spirit: weak hit. -1 momentum for +1 spirit. Momentum 5 → 4.)*

**Beat 6 — The Charge**

Gerald came off the rock at a run, emptying his sidearm to suppress, and closed to knife range. The driver got the rifle around and connected — a hit to Gerald's already-bruised left side — but the plate took most of it and Gerald was already inside the rifle's useful length. The knife found the collar seal. *(Clash +iron: weak hit. Progress 6/10. Endure Harm +iron: strong hit. Health 3 → 1 → 2. In control.)*

**Beat 7 — Finishing It**

The driver went for his sidearm. Gerald stomped the wrist, then cut a large tear in the suit's flank — too large for the auto-seal. As the driver stilled, Gerald noticed the passenger propped against the buggy chassis three metres away, suit damaged from the opening shot but still functional, sidearm raised and shaking. *(Strike +iron: weak hit. Progress 10/10. In a bad spot.)*

**Beat 8 — Clean**

Gerald walked around the buggy without hurrying, came up behind the passenger as he struggled to reposition, and shot him. Two suits on the grey ground, the buggy intact, the junction box twenty minutes up the track. Maria emerged from behind the boulder with her sidearm still raised. *(Take Decisive Action: strong hit. Objective achieved. +1 momentum, 4 → 5.)*
