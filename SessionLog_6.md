**SESSION 6**

**Opening Vignette**

The camera opens on Gerald asleep in the pilot's seat of the cold, powered-down shuttle. Life support at minimum. On the aft bulkhead, the weapons locker sits bolted and stencilled.

**Beat 1 — The Locker**

Gerald opens the weapons locker. Two rifles, six sidearms, and on the bottom shelf a signal jammer he's glad to find. He also equips some ballistic plates in slots for that purpose on his suit. *(Check Your Gear +supply: strong hit with a match. +1 momentum. Momentum 9 → 10.)*

**Beat 2 — Comms Check**

Gerald runs the first live test of the team's comms. All four circle members respond clean inside ninety seconds. He asks who can handle a firearm. Davel: yes, qualified. Sandra: yes, no elaboration needed. Carl: basic, six years ago. Hamzi: no. The blacklist on the monitoring seems to work, the raiders don't react.

**Beat 3 — The Framework**

Gerald lays out the ambush framework over comms. Jammer on before contact. Sandra at the door as the familiar face. Gerald and Davel on the escort. Carl on the route. Hamzi on comms only. Davel describes the terrain — Minka's cabin is a standalone structure on the eastern residential edge, forty metres of vacuum from a maintenance corridor. Gerald tells Sandra to be ready for the possibility things don't go as expected. Sandra stops the plan cold: the raiders won't let the survey arm out of the hub.

**Beat 4 - Davel has a plan**

Gerald hits a wall. Davel breaks in — he can call in a favour from a repair crew contact who knows every maintenance passage between the hub and the residential cluster. Hamzi follows — he'll manufacture a technical problem in the survey arm systems, draw a raider away with him to supervise. The circle solves the problem without being asked.

**Beat 5 — The Crossing**

Gerald makes the crossing from the shuttle to the settlement. He takes his time this time — observes the patrol patterns from the ridge before moving, crosses carefully, reaches the settlement without incident. The thirty minutes costs him but buys him the ground knowledge he needs. *(Face Danger +shadow: weak hit. Lose Momentum (-1). Momentum 9.)*

**Beat 6 — Reading the Ground**

Gerald scouts the eastern residential cluster. Locates Minka's cabin — largest standalone structure on that side, equipment lean-to on the northern face, two airlocks. The lean-to creates a blind spot from the hub approach: good ambush position, clean angle on the main airlock. The complication: a secondary airlock on the eastern face opens directly toward the vacuum gap and Davel's maintenance corridor. If Minka runs, she has options. Gerald notes it. *(Secure an Advantage +wits: weak hit. +1 add banked for Enter the Fray.)*

**Beat 7 — The Jammer**

Gerald places the signal jammer under the porch of Minka's cabin, out of sight. Remote activation. Once on, it stays on until physically retrieved — the circle's own comms go dark in the immediate area the moment it fires.

**Beat 8 — The Circle Arrives**

Hamzi signals at twenty to noon: *"Starting now. Good luck."* Then silence — with a raider, can't talk. The circle arrives twelve minutes later. Gerald briefs them on the lean-to position, the eastern airlock problem, and the comms blackout once the jammer fires. He arms Carl, Sandra and Davel. Carl takes a position at the far end of the approach corridor without being told, watching the hub. Everything is ready. They wait.

---

**End State**

The circle is in position around Minka Brinberg's cabin. Gerald and Davel at the lean-to, Sandra ready to approach the door, Carl watching the hub approach. The jammer is under the porch, remote activation in Gerald's pocket. Hamzi is somewhere in the settlement keeping a raider occupied and off comms. The escort — Minka and two raiders — will arrive from the hub at noon.

The circle does not know Minka is a willing collaborator.
