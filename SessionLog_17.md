**SESSION 17**

**Beat 1 — Waking**

Gerald surfaced to Inara already working on him and already talking — her husband in the jail, the shoulder luckier than it looked, Sandra should have called her sooner. She used Gerald's own kit to treat him, avoiding the settlement dispensary and any record the raiders might track. She cleared the wound and told him to sleep again, and he did. *(Heal +iron: weak hit. Wounded cleared. Health 0 → 2. Supply 4 → 2.)*

**Beat 2 — First Words**

When Gerald next woke, Davel and Sandra were with him, both openly armed. He pushed through the grogginess long enough to tell them what Aran had revealed — a missile was already on the ground, and the raiders planned to use it to drive the Steady Return into a kill zone. Davel didn't follow it all, but he heard the urgency. Gerald lost the thread before he could explain further and went under again.

**Beat 3 — The Circle**

Davel woke him to put him on the comms. The news came in from every direction: Ferris and two miners shot dead at the complex, the miners armed from the cache with Bran now in control. Full curfew at the settlement, a dead-or-alive reward posted for Davel and Sandra. Hamzi had been forced to give the raiders the backup dish route to restore partial mining system access — they had found it and cut it. The Steady Return was now unreachable. Gerald realized they would place the missile at Solomon Mont before the hopelessness took him.

**Beat 4 — Foreman's Fatigue**
Gerald broke. Davel and Sandra were at his side immediately. Davel held him. Sandra opened comms and named it: foreman's fatigue. Then, with Gerald still on the line, the circle made a plan. Sandra laid out the endgame at the settlement — when the missile moved, the raiders' grip would loosen; they would push the mercs out, free Linde, and deal with whatever was left. Carl directed Maria to coordinate with Bran at the complex and move armed miners toward the Mont, and tasked Hamzi with cutting the miners onto their own subnet, out of the raiders' comm oversight. Both confirmed. Maria spoke last, quietly, and gave Gerald his own words back to him — he didn't have to make this right alone; they were making it right together. He stayed on the line, listened to all of it, and eventually went back to sleep with Davel still close. (Sojourn +heart: weak hit. Hearten, automatic strong hit. Spirit 2 → 5.)

**Beat 5 — Morning**

Inara returned the next morning, moving through a settlement locked down hard — residents confined to habs, rations delivered by selected runners, the inn fortified with barricades and a machine gun at the door. She checked the shoulder, found it better, and left. Sandra was there when Gerald woke, and put her hand on his good shoulder without a word. *(Sojourn +heart: weak hit. Heal, automatic strong hit. Health 2 → 4. Momentum 5 → 6.)*

**Beat 6 — The Day**

Good news and bad arrived in turns. Forty armed miners were moving for Solomon Mont in buggies. Davel had two fast bikes on the service track — enough to get Gerald and himself to the Mont in time. The main comms dish was dead, critical components pulled by the raiders; Graff could not be warned. The same cut left the mercs on the ground equally blind, locked to pre-arranged orders with no contact to the ship. Gerald slept through the shuttle's departure.

**Beat 7 — Suited Up**

Inara came one last time, checked the shoulder, said *better* in the tone of someone managing expectations, and left. Gerald put his suit on — methodical, familiar — and got to his feet. The shoulder pulled. He noted it and moved anyway. Davel was ready. The bikes were waiting. On the way out he tossed the pad to Sandra. "Take a look at that when you have a moment".
