Session 4 opens with Gerald, Carl, Davel Oren, and Hamzi in a back room of the settlement's industrial infrastructure. The circle of trust is established. Gerald is carrying the data pad. It logs all traffic passing through the connection Hamzi built between the software specialist and the main mining systems. The raiders do not know it exists.

The raiders are comfortable and careless. Rotating watch on the main hub. The chief surveyor and software specialist are held in the jail. The rest of the settlement is on normal work rotations under supervision.

Key things the circle needs to establish:
- Who on the survey arm has the knowledge to read the pad data, and can they be trusted and reached safely
- Who among the broader settler population has the spine to organise resistance
- What the device the raiders brought actually does

The data pad is the most pressing thread. Whatever is on it will shape the timeline and the plan.
