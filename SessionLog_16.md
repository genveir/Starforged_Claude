**SESSION 16**

**Beat 1 — The Knife**

Gerald lay still on the floor of the ore processing plant, unarmed, and worked out where the knife had gone. He signalled the position to Sandra and she gave him a covering shot, buying him the window. He crossed the floor, pulled the knife from under the belt housing, and charged Aran's position — and found it empty. Aran had moved quietly and Gerald had lost him in the dark. *(React Under Fire +wits: weak hit. Momentum 9 → 8. In a bad spot.)*

**Beat 2 — Under the Sorter**

Gerald reasoned it through fast: not the catwalks, not around the sorter — back and under, where a wounded man could wait with a sightline. He jumped for the top of the sorter as Aran's shot came from underneath it and missed. From above he had the position and Sandra had the angles. Aran was cornered. *(React Under Fire +wits: strong hit. Momentum 8 → 9. In control.)*

**Beat 3 — The Slip**

Gerald talked at Aran from the top of the sorter — the patrols were rolled up, two against one, come out. Aran's nerve cracked just enough: he demanded to know how Gerald had heard about the missile being offloaded, then caught himself. He'd said too much. Gerald hadn't known a missile was moving groundside until that moment. *(Gain Ground +heart: miss, burned momentum 9 → 3. Strong hit with match. +2 momentum, 3 → 5. Progress 4/10. Aran reveals a missile is being offloaded.)*

**Beat 4 — The Knife Again**

Aran came out fast and firing, banking on surprise. Sandra was in position and her shot drove him wide. Gerald came down from the sorter knife-first and put the blade into Aran's side. The merc dropped the sidearm and seized Gerald's wrist and collar, and began slowly turning the knife back toward Gerald's chest. He was the stronger man and he knew it. *(Strike +iron: weak hit. Progress 6/10. In a bad spot.)*

**Beat 5 — Grinding**

Gerald burned everything he had to keep the knife off his chest and lost ground anyway. The tip broke the suit and went in — shallow, but in. Sandra was moving but not fast enough. The knowledge that he was going to die unless something changed settled into Gerald with a cold clarity. *(React Under Fire +iron: weak hit. Health 2 → 1. Endure Stress -1, spirit 5 → 4. Resist +spirit: strong hit. Momentum 5 → 6.)*

**Beat 6 — Holding**

Gerald threw his weight sideways rather than against the blade, deflecting the angle rather than reversing it. It bought nothing except seconds, but Sandra had closed the distance and Aran knew it. Gerald held the knife off his chest with both arms shaking and waited. *(React Under Fire +iron: weak hit. Stay in a bad spot.)*

**Beat 7 — Sandra**

Gerald locked Aran's wrist and threw himself across the man's chest, pinning him, and called the shot. Sandra fired point blank. The round went through Aran's chest and clipped Gerald's shoulder on the way out. Gerald pushed himself upright and his arm didn't respond correctly. Aran was dead. *(Strike +iron: weak hit. Progress 8/10. Health 1 → 0. Endure Harm resist +iron: miss. Momentum 6 → 4. Mark wounded.)*

**Beat 8 — Down**

Gerald got one hand on an equipment housing, stood, and the plant tilted sideways. He sat down hard and didn't get back up. Sandra caught him before he went all the way down. Gerald tried to catalogue his injuries and the list dissolved. Sandra told him to stay with her in a voice that was very steady. He wasn't sure he answered her before the darkness came up and took him. *(Take Decisive Action: weak hit. Objective achieved. Endure Stress -2, spirit 4 → 2.)*
