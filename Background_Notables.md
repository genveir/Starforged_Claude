# Notable Characters

## Pre-exodus

Solomon Raiman was a celebrated early Forge surveyor, active decades before the exodus, credited with identifying numerous high-value planets and moons that became the foundation of several prosperous settlements. He surveyed the moon now known as Raiman's Folly and dismissed it as commercially unviable. Later surveyors disagreed, settled it anyway, and named it after him as a dry joke. Raiman himself is long dead. His reputation among professional surveyors remains substantial despite the error — one miss in a long career of finds is not enough to undo a legacy in a place where good survey work is genuinely precious.
