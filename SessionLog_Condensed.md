# Session History (Compressed)

**Long Term Summary**
Bormann's death was personal and Gerald swore the vow because of that. The circle assembled around him fast: Davel and Carl through existing trust, Hamzi through initiative that preceded Gerald's arrival, Sandra through a bargain to save her friend Minka. The ambush killed both mercenaries and Sandra shot Minka who turned out to be a collaborator, which broke Sandra. Gerald felt guilt and accepted his share of responsibility for a plan that moved too fast. Gerald reached the mining complex alone and on foot after surviving a powercell explosion, where Maria recognised him as The Lawman from Spinfeld. The circle made full contact with Sandra stable and the ruse holding; Gerald and Sandra reached stable ground after a direct apology, and the circle began moving toward a plan involving the data cable — but nothing was decided at that point.

**Session 10**
Hamzi found the comms tap — a redirect, not a block — and bypassed it in software, routing Gerald's traffic to the backup dish at the complex. Gerald and Maria retrieved the dish and mounted it at the relay tower on the crater rim. Gerald re-established contact with Graff. The circle debated the cable plan and the ore launcher; the launcher was ruled out, the cable remains open. Gerald took stock of the people around him and found them good.

**Session 11**
Gerald re-established contact with Graff and reported the ground picture. The supply ship's situation made the passive option untenable — three weeks of rations forced the decision to act. The plan took shape: Paul Ortoff takes the shuttle into the interference layer as a decoy insertion, Gerald blows the data cable from the mine side with no local fingerprints. Gerald and Maria returned to the complex under routine cover without incident. Gerald and Maria bonded — she told him he was exactly what she expected, and he told her the job was about collecting people, not one man acting alone.
