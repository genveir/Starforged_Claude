# Session History (Compressed)

**Long Term Summary**
Bormann's death was personal and Gerald swore the vow because of that. The circle assembled around him fast: Davel and Carl through existing trust, Hamzi through initiative that preceded Gerald's arrival, Sandra through a bargain to save Minka from the raider, not knowing she was a willing collaborator. The data pad Sandra read told them the raiders were never here for the minerals — something is buried under the moon, and the minerals were the map. During the rescue operation Sandra shot Minka in a moment of shock and grief, killing someone she had worked alongside for years. Gerald had pushed the plan through faster than the information warranted and knew it. What he owed Sandra was the apology without deflection, and he gave it. Gerald traveled to the mining complex; Maria dropped her wariness the moment she placed his face, because he was the lawman in her district growing up. He reached Graff after days of silence and felt the weight of it lift when the voice came through.

**Session 11**
Gerald re-established contact with Graff and reported the ground picture. The supply ship's situation made the passive option untenable — three weeks of rations forced the decision to act. The plan took shape: Paul Ortoff takes the shuttle into the interference layer as a decoy insertion, Gerald blows the data cable from the mine side with no local fingerprints. Gerald and Maria returned to the complex under routine cover without incident. Gerald and Maria bonded — she told him he was exactly what she expected, and he told her the job was about collecting people, not one man acting alone.

**Session 12**
Gerald found the explosives magazine occupied by a work crew under Ferris and could not act without involving them. He stepped out and laid the full situation in front of Ferris and the miners — the blockade, the rations, the timeline, the jail. Ferris acknowledged he had made the wrong call and committed to stand with Gerald when called. The miners loaded the buggy with what Gerald needed without being asked twice. Ferris agreed to bring Bran around.
