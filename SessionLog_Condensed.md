# Session History (Compressed)

**Long Term Summary**
Bormann's death was personal and Gerald swore the vow because of that. The circle assembled around him fast: Davel and Carl through existing trust, Hamzi through initiative that preceded Gerald's arrival, Sandra through a bargain to save Minka from the raider, not knowing she was a willing collaborator. The data pad Sandra read told them the raiders were never here for the minerals — something is buried under the moon, and the minerals were the map. During the rescue operation Sandra shot Minka in a moment of shock and grief, killing someone she had worked alongside for years. Gerald had pushed the plan through faster than the information warranted and knew it. What he owed Sandra was the apology without deflection, and he gave it. Gerald traveled to the mining complex; Maria dropped her wariness the moment she placed his face, because he was the lawman in her district growing up. He reached Graff after days of silence and felt the weight of it lift when the voice came through. The supply situation forced the decision to act — passive options were gone. Gerald told Maria the job was about collecting people, not one man acting alone; she told him he was exactly what she expected. Gerald brought Ferris across by laying the full situation in front of him without deflection — the blockade, the timeline, the jail — and Ferris acknowledged he had made the wrong call.

**Session 13**
Two mercenaries on patrol intercepted Gerald and Maria on the service track before they reached the cable junction. Gerald killed both; Maria flanked under fire despite having no combat experience. Gerald felt the familiar weight of putting an untrained person into danger and accepted it as the cost of the work.

**Session 14**
The buggy axle gave on the service track and stranded the cargo short of the junction. Paul went off script without being asked — came in low, picked them up, and offered to sell the commando story. The cable junction was blown and Aran's shuttle got eyes on them leaving but didn't catch them. Gerald called the circle after; three acknowledgements came back clean. Hamzi didn't answer. Gerald held the line open a few extra seconds, then closed it.
