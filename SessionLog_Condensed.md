# Session History (Compressed)

**Session 1**
The Steady Return arrived in system to find an unregistered combat vessel in orbit and the settlement under occupation. Hamzi got a whispered message out on a private line: Bormann was dead, the lead surveyor imprisoned, the settlers controlled and afraid. Gerald swore a cordon vow on the bridge and began planning a surface insertion. The raider's two guided missiles were identified as the primary constraint on the Steady Return's ability to act.

**Session 2**
Gerald descended in the shuttle, running cold and silent through the metallicity interference layer. A miscalculated arc forced an improvised burn that cost fuel. He crossed to the settlement on foot, observed patrol patterns, and slipped inside through an unlocked maintenance hatch. A mining foreman he recognised by face led him through the infrastructure to Carl Inswich. Gerald noticed he was unable to raise the Steady Return.

**Session 3**
Gerald laid out the situation to Carl and Davel Oren — the foreman who had brought him in and stayed — and the circle of trust took shape. Hamzi arrived shortly after, having just completed the connection the raiders ordered between the software specialist and the mining systems. Before doing so he installed a listener on the line, invisible to anyone who hadn't laid the cables himself, and handed Gerald the logging data pad.

**Session 4**
Davel recommended Sandra Vael as the only person who could read the pad's survey data — a survey team lead, quiet, iron core. Gerald crossed to the inn to find her. Sandra agreed to help on a condition: she wanted Gerald for an ambush on Minka Brinberg's escort visit to her cabin the following day. Gerald agreed despite the plan having problems. Sandra read the pad and told him the truth of it: the raiders weren't here for the minerals. The minerals were the map. Something is buried under this moon.

**Session 5**
Gerald met Hamzi privately and learned the monitoring system was already compromised in the circle's favour — Hamzi is root, and the raiders only think they have admin access. All five circle members were added to a blacklist before the night was out. Gerald's cover identity in the settlement system remained unresolved. He spent the night in the shuttle. Earlier that evening, Gerald had his first sight of Aran Chrion at the inn — the merc sergeant, broad and unhurried, who cleared a table with nothing but his presence and the room went carefully loud again.

**Session 6**
Gerald armed the circle from the shuttle's weapons locker and briefed the ambush plan. Sandra identified a problem — the survey arm is watched too closely — and Davel solved it without being asked, calling in a repair crew contact and a maintenance route. Gerald scouted Minka's cabin, placed the signal jammer under the porch, and the circle took position. They did not yet know Minka was a willing collaborator.

**Session 7**
The escort arrived. The circle killed both mercenaries cleanly. Inside the cabin, Minka talked — she had bought into the raiders' purpose entirely, driven by scientific zeal at what was buried under the moon, and had been planning to leave with them. Sandra listened without expression and then shot her. The aftermath was ugly: Sandra turned the weapon on Gerald before he talked her down, and ended up too broken to return to the settlement. Davel took her to a hidden compartment in the mining infrastructure. Gerald sent the shuttle back to the Steady Return on autonomous flight — establishing the fiction of an outside commando — and walked to the mines alone.

**Session 8**
Gerald crossed the surface to the mining complex on foot, surviving a powercell explosion en route that left him bruised down his left side. He raised Davel from a crater rim relay tower and was brought inside by buggy. Maria introduced herself and gave him the local picture. When he pulled off his helmet she recognised him as The Lawman from Spinfeld.
