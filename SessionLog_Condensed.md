# Session History (Compressed)

**Long Term Summary**
Bormann's death was personal and Gerald swore the vow because of that. The circle assembled around him faster than he had any right to expect: Davel and Carl through existing trust, Hamzi through initiative that preceded Gerald's arrival, Sandra through a bargain to save her friend Minka. The ambush killed both mercenaries and Sandra shot Minka who turned out to be a collaborator, but broke Sandra. Gerald felt guilt and he has accepted his share of responsibility for a plan that moved too fast. Gerald reached the mining complex alone and on foot after surviving a powercell explosion, where Maria recognised him as The Lawman from Spinfeld.

**Session 9**
The circle made full contact for the first time, with Sandra stable and the ruse that no locals were involved holding at the complex. Gerald apologised to Sandra directly and they reached stable ground. The circle took stock: the raiders have tightened exterior security but have not looked inward. Maria surfaced the miners' hidden weapons cache, and the circle began moving toward a plan involving the data cable and mining explosives — but nothing is decided.

**Session 10**
Hamzi found the comms tap — a redirect, not a block — and bypassed it in software, routing Gerald's traffic to the backup dish at the complex. Gerald and Maria retrieved the dish and mounted it at the relay tower on the crater rim. Gerald re-established contact with Graff. The circle debated the cable plan and the ore launcher; the launcher was ruled out, the cable remains open. Gerald took stock of the people around him and found them good.
