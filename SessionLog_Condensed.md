# Session History (Compressed)

**Long Term Summary**
Bormann's death was personal — a real friend, not a colleague — and Gerald swore the vow because of that, not in spite of it. The circle assembled around him faster than he had any right to expect: Davel and Carl through existing trust, Hamzi through initiative that preceded Gerald's arrival, Sandra through a bargain that cost her more than she showed. The ambush killed both mercenaries and Minka, but broke Sandra — she turned a weapon on Gerald before he talked her down, and he has accepted his share of responsibility for a plan that moved too fast. Gerald reached the mining complex alone and on foot, where Maria recognised him as The Lawman from Spinfeld.

**Session 8**
Gerald crossed the surface on foot, surviving a powercell explosion that left him bruised down his left side. Maria brought him inside by buggy and gave him the local picture. When he pulled off his helmet she recognised him as The Lawman from Spinfeld.

**Session 9**
The circle made full contact for the first time, with Sandra stable and the ruse holding at the complex. Gerald apologised to Sandra directly and they reached stable ground. The circle took stock: the raiders have tightened exterior security but have not looked inward. Maria surfaced the miners' hidden weapons cache, and the circle began moving toward a plan involving the data cable and mining explosives — but nothing is decided.
