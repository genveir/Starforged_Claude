**SESSION 5**

**Opening Vignette**

A raider sits alone in the comms hub, feet up on the dashboard, clicking through recorded settler conversations with comfortable boredom. He catches two settlers complaining to each other — they don't know how long the occupation will last, don't even know what the raiders want. He grins to himself.

---

**Beat 1 — Dinner at the Inn**

Early evening, just past 1900. Gerald and Sandra eat together at the inn. Sandra confirms the escort window for Minka Brinberg's cabin visit is noon tomorrow, giving the circle the morning to prepare. Gerald raises the comms situation — he's been dark since landing, unwilling to put a new transmitter on the network. Sandra confirms the standard channels are compromised and points him toward Hamzi, who takes his evening meal late.

**Beat 2 — Gerald asks about comms**

Gerald keeps his voice low and lays out his concern to Sandra. She confirms the settlers' working assumption: everything on the main comm infrastructure is being recorded. Hamzi is the man to talk to. She expects him shortly.

**Beat 3 — Aran Chrion**

A merc — broad, unhurried, with the particular quality of someone who uses cruelty and discomfort as a tool — walks into the inn with two companions. He clears a table with nothing but his presence, settlers gathering their trays and finding somewhere else to be without a word being said. The room goes quiet, then carefully loud again. Twenty minutes later Hamzi comes through the door, finds a spot at the counter, doesn't look at Gerald or Sandra.

**Beat 4 — The arrangement / the corridor**

Gerald drifts over to Hamzi and asks quietly if there's somewhere they can talk. Hamzi names cable junction seven, maintenance level, east corridor. Twenty minutes. Gerald leaves first, passing two raiders in the corridor outside the hub. One of them looks at him long enough to file the face away. Gerald doesn't break stride. *(Face Danger +heart: weak hit. Momentum 9 → 8.)*

**Beat 5 — Cable junction seven**

Gerald arrives at the junction. Hamzi follows, checks the corridor, pulls the access panel closed. Conduit runs and junction boxes, the hum of the settlement's infrastructure around them. Hamzi waits.

**Beat 6 — Root access**

Gerald lays out the problem: five people spread across an occupied settlement who need to talk without the raiders cutting in. Hamzi laughs. The raiders only think they have admin access because the system told them so — the system lied. Hamzi is root. The monitoring system already doesn't flag conversations he participates in. Adding Carl, Sandra, Davel, and Gerald to the blacklist is trivial. One constraint: stay out of earshot of raiders. A live conversation they overhear won't get flagged, and they'll notice. Everything else is handled.

**Beat 7 — The identity problem**

Gerald raises the next issue: he needs a recognised identity in the system for the blacklist to function, and for his presence in the settlement to have any cover. Hamzi can add him to the comm system but doesn't have access to the deeper admin infrastructure — berths, arrivals, manifests. The innkeeper could register him as a late arrival, plausibly enough. The software specialist could do it too, but she's in the jail. For tonight, the shuttle is cleaner than trying to explain a berth he doesn't have. Hamzi will have all five names on the blacklist before he sleeps.

**Beat 8 — The walk back**

Gerald leaves the settlement for the shuttle. Stealth rather than confidence — walking away from a settlement toward hidden terrain at night reads differently than walking toward it. He nearly doesn't make it. A patrol running off-pattern comes close enough that he kills his suit lights and goes flat on the ground, unable to see or hear anything. He lies still until he's sure they must be gone. By the time he reaches the shuttle his hands are steady. The rest of him takes longer. *(Face Danger +shadow: weak hit. Endure Stress -1. Spirit 5 → 4.)*

---

**End State**

Gerald is in the shuttle, cold and dark, one kilometre from the settlement. Secure comms are established — all five members of the circle are on Hamzi's blacklist. Gerald's identity in the settlement system remains unresolved; the innkeeper is the morning's first task. The escort ambush on Minka Brinberg's cabin visit is at noon. Fifteen hours, give or take.

**Mechanical updates:** Momentum 8. Spirit 4. Hamzi connection progress 3/10.
