**SESSION 15**

**Opening Vignette**

On the bridge of the raider vessel, the three officers assessed their situation. Tirza laid out the tactical picture bluntly — their intelligence was degrading, the Steady Return's shuttle was too stealthy to track, and the commandos were outclassing the mercenary complement. They determined that it is likely a black ops cordon navy team that has somehow learned about their operation. Tom pushed for executing hostages to send a message; Tirza shut it down on practical grounds, noting that fourteen soldiers couldn't hold the hub against settlers who hadn't actually surrendered their weapons. The discussion resolved on Tom's plan: move a missile to the surface outside the settlement under heavy guard, use it to chase the Steady Return into a kill zone. The officer approved it. *(Begin a Session: vignette spotlights a danger. +1 momentum, 9 → 10.)*

**Beat 1 — Back on the Ground**

A text message came through before dawn: the raider shuttle had returned during the night, and Hamzi was still silent. Gerald had Paul fly them in low, hugging the surface, parking behind the ridge well clear of the anti-air cannon. He said goodbye to Maria and Paul and walked the rest of the way alone. *(No roll.)*

**Beat 2 — The Ridge**

Gerald took the same position he'd held on his first day on the moon and gave the settlement a full half hour. The raider shuttle was groundside now, sitting on the landing pad, which was new. Patrols had shifted to pairs only, intervals tighter. No settlers outside at all. He mapped a route through to the maintenance passage entry on the eastern side and started down. *(Secure an Advantage +wits: weak hit. +1 on next move.)*

**Beat 3 — Caught**

Gerald threaded two patrols cleanly. He made the maintenance passage entry and got the hatch closed behind him — and found Aran waiting in the dark. The merc told him the backup dish at the relay tower was compromised, and from Paul's check-in transmission they'd realized Gerald was on the ground at the settlement. He had set the trap himself, patrols covering the door and the ridge outside. Gerald was caught cold. *(Face Danger +shadow: miss. Pay the Price.)*

---

**Beat 4 — Disarmed**

Aran had him at gunpoint and walked him through surrendering the rifle and sidearm, enjoying it. Gerald complied, careful and deliberate, right hand going to the floor with the pistol — and left hand finding the knife. He drove forward before Aran finished his next sentence, the blade catching the merc across the forearm and his shoulder slamming him into the passage wall. Aran didn't go down but he was off balance and bleeding. *(Enter the Fray +iron: weak hit. In control.)*

**Beat 5 — Sandra**

Gerald pressed the advantage and put the knife into Aran's shoulder — through the suit, deep. Aran roared and shoved him hard into the opposing wall, left side first, pain flaring white across Gerald's bruised flank. As Gerald slid down the wall a shot cracked through the passage. Sandra had heard the fight from Davel's hidden compartment, come out, picked up Gerald's sidearm from the floor, and put a round past Aran's head. The merc calculated fast and went backwards through a doorway. *(Strike +iron: strong hit with a match. Progress 2/10. +1 on next move.)*

---

**Beat 6 — The Jammer**

Gerald went through the door after him without waiting for Sandra, tossing the signal jammer into the ore processing plant as he crossed the threshold and flipping it on mid-swing. Aran's call for backup died in the void a second too late. Gerald wrenched the knife free from his shoulder and drove a knee into his sternum, sending him staggering back into an equipment housing in the low gravity. Aran didn't go down. His good hand found his sidearm. *(Gain Ground +wits: strong hit. Progress 3/10. +1 on next move.)*

---

**Beat 7 — The Boot**

Sandra came through the door with the gun up. Gerald caught her eye, read the steadiness there, and charged straight at the rising sidearm. Her shots drove Aran sideways behind a heavy equipment housing — but he got there in time, and his boot caught Gerald flush in the face as he came in low. The knife skittered away in the dark. Gerald hit the floor and stayed down deliberately, tasting blood, blinking the stars back. Aran was behind cover, still breathing, still armed. Sandra had gone silent somewhere in the dark behind him. *(Strike +heart: miss. In a bad spot. Endure Harm -1, health 2 → 1. Resist +iron: weak hit. -1 momentum, health 1 → 2. Momentum 9.)*
